Future

UCLA has had a tremendous impact on its students, faculty, Los Angeles, California and the world. Our institution’s mission to create, disseminate, preserve and apply our knowledge for the betterment of our global society will continue to live on for generations to come. While 100 years have gone by, UCLA has yet much more to offer in the future.
