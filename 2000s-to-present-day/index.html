UCLA Community School

The UCLA Community School is one of 10 locally governed public schools in the greater Los Angeles area. It officially opened in 2009 in the Koreatown-Picto district, serving primarily Korean and Spanish speaking families. The school has a dual-language, college preparation program.¬†
