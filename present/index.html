Creator Testimonial 

Here’s a testimonial from one of our creators, Karinn, about why she chose UCLA! 

UCLA had been my dream school since I was 7. Both of my parents went to UCLA for graduate school, so growing up I attended Bruin Woods, UCLA’s Alumni Family Camp in Lake Arrowhead. Bruin Woods was my first exposure to the Bruin Community and I of course wanted to be just like my counselors (who were current UCLA students) when I grew up. Years later when college application season rolled around, UCLA was still on my mind but I wasn’t sure if I wanted to go to college so close to home. In the end, my decision came down between UCLA and one other school. Both schools were academically prestigious, had a great location, and presented great post-grad opportunities, but what I wanted more than anything was a school where I thought I could thrive academically and where students felt happy and supported. When I came to Bruin Day, UCLA’s admitted student day, it was apparent how happy the students were and I realized UCLA was still my dream school. I went home and signed my SIR, and couldn’t be happier with my decision. I like to say UCLA was my dream school and these four years have been nothing short of a dream. I am so thankful for my friends and faculty that have supported me these past few years and helped me grow into the person I am today.
